  1. (a+b)2=a2+2ab+b2(a+b)^2=a^2+2ab+b^2(a+b)2=a2+2ab+b2
  2. (a−b)2=a2−2ab+b2(a-b)^2=a^2-2ab+b^2(a−b)2=a2−2ab+b2
